About

I am a Ph.D. student at the Department of Agricultural, Environmental, and Development Economics at The Ohio State University (Columbus, OH), which I was granted a Master of Science in 2025. Besides, I also hold a Master of Science in Forest Economics from Mississippi State University (Starkville, MS) and a Bachelor in Economics from the University of São Paulo, Brazil.

My work centers on environmental, climate change, and development economics, with experience in operational research, causal inference, and agricultural market analysis.
